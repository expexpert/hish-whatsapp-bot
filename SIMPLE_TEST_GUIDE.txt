# WHATSAPP BOT: SIMPLE TESTING GUIDE (Copy/Paste Ready)

---------------------------------------------------------
1. MAIN DASHBOARD & STATUS (Multilingual)
---------------------------------------------------------
Send these words to check your totals and set language:
*   English: Status, Balance, Dashboard
*   Français: Statut, Solde, Tableau, Compte
-> Expect: Income, Total Expenses, Balance, and VAT totals.

---------------------------------------------------------
1.5 MAIN MENU (Multilingual)
---------------------------------------------------------
Send these words to open the menu and set language:
*   English: Menu, Start, Home
*   Français: Menu, Bonjour, Salut, Début

---------------------------------------------------------
2. QUICK REPORTS MENU
---------------------------------------------------------
Send 'Report' (EN) or 'Rapport' (FR) to open the menu.

* Monthly Summary: Should show Income/Expenses for April 2026.
* Unpaid Invoices: Should only show invoices marked as "Unpaid".
* Search by Name: Type 'Carrefour' to see how much you spent there.
* Recent Activity: Use the buttons at the bottom of the menu to jump straight to your most recent clients or suppliers.

---------------------------------------------------------
3. EXPENSES (MONEY OUT)
---------------------------------------------------------
Copy and paste these to check the AI:
* EN: I spent 150 MAD at Carrefour for groceries today via cash
* FR: J'ai payé 150 MAD chez Carrefour pour les courses aujourd'hui en espèces
-> Expect: Bot confirms 150 Amount, Carrefour Entity, and Groceries Category.

---------------------------------------------------------
4. INVOICES (MONEY IN)
---------------------------------------------------------
Copy and paste these to check revenue:
* EN: Billed my client Google 2500 MAD for software work
* FR: Facturé 2500 MAD au client Google pour travaux informatiques
-> Expect: Total Income (Revenue) increases by 2500.

---------------------------------------------------------
5. DRILLING DOWN (PERIOD SELECTION)
---------------------------------------------------------
Search for a name (e.g., 'Amazon') and then try these:
* Click 'This Month': Shows spending from April 1st.
* Click 'Last Month': Shows spending from March.
* Click 'All Time': Shows the total lifetime sum for that name.

---------------------------------------------------------
6. DOCUMENT LISTS & CARDS
---------------------------------------------------------
1. Run a report -> Click 'View List'.
2. See the list of the last 10 documents.
3. Click on a specific item in that list.
4. Expect: The bot sends a "Summary Card" for that specific document.

---------------------------------------------------------
7. SPECIALIZED ENTITIES (NITESH ARYA)
---------------------------------------------------------
Test priority drill-downs (AI sensing):

*   SPECIFIC STATUS: 
    - EN: "status for nitesh arya"
    - FR: "statut pour nitesh arya"
    -> Expect: Financial summary specifically for Nitesh Arya.

*   FILTERED REPORT: 
    - EN: "report for nitesh arya"
    - FR: "rapport pour nitesh arya"
    -> Expect: Drill-down report options for Nitesh Arya.

*   MONTHLY SEARCH: 
    - EN: "status of month january"
    - FR: "statut du mois de janvier"
    -> Expect: Full dashboard summary for January (any year).

*   COMPLEX DRILL: 
    - EN: "report for nitesh arya january"
    - FR: "rapport pour nitesh arya en janvier"
    -> Expect: Combined filter (Nitesh Arya + January).

---------------------------------------------------------
8. VOICE NOTES & ERRORS
---------------------------------------------------------
* Voice EN: "I spent fifty dollars for coffee at Starbucks."
* Error Test: Send 'Lunch at KFC' (no price).
-> Expect: Bot should ask "How much did it cost?".
